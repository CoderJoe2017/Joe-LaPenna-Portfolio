How I got started. The early stuff.

Since I was 14 I was interested in Coding. I started with Basic, then went onto Visual Basic and C++. I became a computer tech for a number of years building custom systems for a variety of clients and businesses. I still dabbled in coding while I was following my career as an effects artist. I started doing computer graphics in the 90s using Softimage, Maya and 3dsmax. I coded many scripts for those platforms. during the years I have created many web pages for various clients and industries and along the way became an expert Photoshop user. I now am following through with expanding my knowledge of web development and design. I am currently undertaking several courses to get myself up to date. I am now working with HTML, CSS, Node JS, Bootstrap, I love anything to do with frontend and backend development. I am also versed in GoLang, Python, Kotlin, Javascript, C# and Lua. I love what I do and am always up for a challenge.

Graphic Design Experience. The long road.

I began working with Photoshop in 1989. Since then I have created thousands of custom images for vasious clients and scenarios. It was an easy fit for me since I have been a traditional artist since I was 7 years old. Since I picked up a pencil I knew I wanted to be an artist. I have since become an expert user and also digitally sculpt using different programs and then output the files to my 3d printer.
